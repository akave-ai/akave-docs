[Akave](https://www.akave.ai/) was founded by a team of key leaders from [Protocol Labs](https://www.protocol.ai/) and a group of passionate developers, emerging as a spin-out from [Protocol Labs](https://www.protocol.ai/). With deep industry insights and technical expertise in Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks (DePIN), [Akave](https://www.akave.ai/) is dedicated to building a scalable and secure Layer 2 storage chain.

We provide seamless data management services that empower DePIN applications, decentralized application (DApp) builders, Layer 1 and Layer 2 blockchains, and Web2 users to transition into Web3 with ease. By leveraging our long-standing experience in decentralized infrastructure, [Akave](https://www.akave.ai/) offers a robust and reliable data management layer designed to simplify and enhance data handling in the decentralized future.
