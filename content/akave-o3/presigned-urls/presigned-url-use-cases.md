- Secure downloads for customer portals or dashboards
- Public access to data without making the whole bucket public
- Uploading content from external sources (mobile apps, edge devices, etc.)
- Time-restricted content access for events, training, or temporary distribution

Presigned URLs are a lightweight way to grant temporary access while keeping your credentials secure and access tightly scoped.
